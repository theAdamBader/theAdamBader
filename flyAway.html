Game Overview

What is the game?

Fly Away depicts the story of a celestial void that that has to face their own demons. Stuck on an endless of enemies, it must fight and defeat their demons to find true tranqulity.

Why create this game?

Fly Away is a shoot 'em up game which you play as the void and have to face a wave of dark voids. Reason for creating this game is because having to face your own demons is a challenge and so facing and conquering your own demons is an achievement. Also, no one thought of making a game with a meaning behind it and I wanted to do something out of the norm.

Gameplay

The gameplay is near at its core mechanics. There are at basics aspects of a where you can move and shoot your enemies also having a health and score system for each time you, the player, kill an enemy.

Contribution

Player, Enemies & Others

Player and enemies were created using Adobe Illustrator so that it have smooth edges as Illustrator is useful for creating a vector images while Photoshop deals with pixels

Adding bullets, health and entities were useful as with entities it made it easy to give the player and enemy class similar traits.
