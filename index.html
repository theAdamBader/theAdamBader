ABOUT ME

ADAM BADER

A Programmer, Game Developer & Artist

My name is Adam Bader, I was born on the 25th of April in 1989. I am a gender non-conforming individual who just finished studies at Goldsmiths, University of London, BSc Games Programming. I have been developing games for the past 3 years. I love coding, drawing and the colour turquoise. This site is a presentation of my most recent and favourite projects. Fill free to contact me using the social icons below.

Pronouns: They/Them
